ReadMe.md
This is a simple README file for the project.

Tech Stack:
Language : C++
Rendering window: raylib
spritesheet: 32x32 tiles (exceptions)


This game is about following:
A surival rougelike with two modes: Survival/Adventure Mode And Battle Mode
    - Generational Lineage System
    - Genetics-based stats that's varied on RNG (Random Number Generator) and starting stats
    - Survival needs: Food, water, sleep and temperature
    - Kenshi-inspired open world with chunked map loading (with specific areas that are pre-made)
    - Training system: Cycling (Different training methods (Training is self training, swimming or mining)(Meditation is meditation gains)(Sparring is sparring with another npc or an object/wall)(Focus is firing ki blasts, beams or energy moves)
    - Size system: Small(-endurance, -strength, +speed, +offense, +defense), Medium, Large(+endurance, +strength, -speed, -offense, -defense)
    - Saiyan Classes: Low Class (Higher anger and lower transformation requirments), Middle Class and Elite (Lower anger and higher transformation requirments but higher powerlevel rate when training)

Battle Mode:
    Battle occurs when player attacks another NPC (that aren't certain animal NPCs) or a hostile NPC. A loading screen cutscene will play and the battle will begin.
    - Battle is turn-based
    - Battle is 1v1
    - Battle is 2v2
    - Battle is 3v3
    - Battle is 4v4
    - Battle is 5v5
    - Battle is 6v6
    - Battle is 7v7
    Or 1 Vs the entire enemy team depending on team size. Solo fighters gain more but more vulnerable to being KOed and KO events can happen (Broken body part, tail removals, losing limbs and dying(another RNG event where if the player survives, they get death zenkai)).
